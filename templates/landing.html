Welcome to Project TEDDY

A Smart Companion for Comfort, Connection, and Care

Emotional support, whenever it's needed most.

Project TEDDY is an innovative AI-powered therapeutic teddy bear designed to provide comfort, empathy, and emotional regulation to people of all ages. From calming heartbeats to kind, understanding conversations—TEDDY is always there.

Get Started Learn More

Always There

24/7 emotional support

Why Project TEDDY?

In a world where mental health support isn't always accessible, TEDDY bridges the gap. Whether you're a child facing big feelings, a teen navigating stress, or an elderly loved one feeling isolated—TEDDY offers a soft touch and a caring voice.

What TEDDY Can Do

Voice & Touch Detection

Detect emotions through voice and touch
